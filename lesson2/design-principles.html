Visual Hierarchy

GitHub

GitHub Homepage

The words "Lets build from here, together" appears to be more important than the text beneath. This visual representation appears to show other items being more important than the other related items.This does a great job in grabbing the attention of site visitors and makes content consumption easier.

Proximity

Torgerson Design Patners

TDP Homepage

The visual representation of the company name, "Design Partners", and the small words beneath that look like they belong together. The visual relationship between these elements is important because it creates less clutter and presents a more organized layout. It provides a focal point and excellent user experience when navigating and learning about the company and its services.

Alignment

Simply Chocolate

GitHub Homepage

Alignment also helps in archieving a visual relationship between elements. There is a great representation of this principle in the header of this website. From the chocolate option to the option to shop is a presentation of left alignment. The strategic placement of different items provides a flow to the website and easy navigation for site users.
