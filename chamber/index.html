White-water rafting on the Zambezi River is one of the best in the world, and this 2 day festival is a celebration of the fun and rapids that you will find in Victoria Falls. Teams of six compete in a variety of challenges for the title of Zambezi Champion! The Pumping Legs for Water is usually a 2 day event where mountain bikers ride through parts of Hwange National Park for the purpose of raising funds to maintain and develop water sources inside the national park.

News

Victoria Falls, this amazing Adventure Capital of Africa - has so much to offer! Not only is it a natural wonder of the world and renowned for having some of the best activities to be found anywhere on our planet, it now has some of the most spectacular and exciting events to boot.We have rafting, mountain biking and a marathon!Throughout the year there are many exciting and interesting activities and Victoria Falls events not all requiring us to be 'Ironmen'! And then at the end of the year there's the annual Vic Falls Carnival- an entertainment packed New Year's celebration, with live music from some of the tops bands around, even from across the borders
