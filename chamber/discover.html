Experience the exhilerating Victoria Falls cultural dances that will leave you wanting more! Each week the Rainbow Hotel is braced with different culture dance groups that teaches about culture through dance and dramarization. Zimbabwe has a rich culture that is perfectly represented through dance and dramarization hence Victoria Falls is the perfect spot to soak yourself in the culture. A mixture of different tribal dances provides unique but similar culture experience.

The adventure continues with the breathtaking safari rides and see the most amazing and fascinating animals including the big 5!Imagine coming face to face with a lion and being able to cuddle the cubs! Come enjoy the awesome animals that thrive living in the great Victoria Falls.

Watch the sun slip below the horizon on a 2-hour cruise of the Zambezi river. Enjoy cocktails and hors doeuvres on deck as Zimbabwe's diverse animals flock to the water's edge. See flocks of birds as they swoop and glide across the sky, soak up the last rays of African sunshine and slow down to the gentle pace of life on the Zambezi.

Bungee Jump off the famous Victoria Falls Bridge. Experience 111 meters of free-fall and 4 seconds of pure adrenalin! If the sheer excitement of a bungee isn't enough, this must be the most beautiful bungee on the planet. A spectacular backdrop of the epic Victoria Falls sets the scene. Move to the edge of the bridge, arms out and 5-4-3-2-1- BUNGEE. Leap off the bridge and plummet towards the rapids of the mighty Zambezi River and let the adrenaline flow.

Enjoy a half-day rafting the Zambezi river with a local guide and admire the landscape of Batoka Gorge. Flowing through a shallow basin, the river winds below flat plateau as far as the eye can see. Choose to raft a more challenging section of the river with class four and five rapids, or opt for a more beginner-friendly section. After the trip, lunch (included) is served.
